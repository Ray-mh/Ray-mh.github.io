The Passage Within

2017-2018 Rhino 3D, Lumion, AutoCAD, Photoshop

This architectural design tells the emotional journey of a person moving through five stages of life — from innocence to self-realization. It begins with "Childhood," a space symbolizing freedom and openness. As people grow and leave behind the carefree nature of childhood, they enter "Transformation," a phase of change that gradually draws them toward darker, more complex emotional states. This leads to "Depression," where the space represents heaviness, isolation, and inner struggle. After enduring this darkness, the individual experiences "Confusion," a transitional space where they search for direction and clarity, confronting the inner turmoil in hopes of finding a way out. Finally, through reflection and acceptance, they arrive at "Healing," a space of light, balance, and renewal — symbolizing restoration, growth, and the ability to continue life with newfound strength.

The Reconciliation Neighborhood

2017-2018 Rhino 3D, Lumion, AutoCAD, Photoshop

Contemporary cities often evolve toward modernity, sometimes overlooking their historical and cultural identities. In Iran, the traditional Mahaleh was a small neighborhood defined by its distinctive architectural character and communal layout. This project focused on designing a 'University Staff Neighborhood' inspired by the Mahaleh concept, reinterpreting its cultural and spatial qualities to create a modern yet contextually rooted living environment.

Heritage

Digitization Of Historical Safavid Towers

2020 Rhino 3D, Photoshop, Procreate

The twin towers of Safa is a "Kabutar-Khaneh", which means house of a pigeon, in Iran. It is designed to protect pigeons from predators. The inside of the structure has a parametric geometry. I attempted to digitalize a 3D section of the building to showcase its geometrical patterns based on its pictures and plans.
