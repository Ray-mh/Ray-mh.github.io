About Me

Ray

I bring a diverse set of skills and experiences that extend beyond any single job title. My passion lies in connecting art, technology, psychology, architecture, and cultural history. With four years of experience across various design roles and my current position as a Digital Transformation Design Trainee at the Digital School Society, I'm driven by the intersection of creativity and innovation. I love learning from the past, leveraging today's tools, and shaping what's next. I thrive in helping studios bring bold, avant-garde ideas to life—observing deeply, thinking visually, and sharing ideas through presentations that are simple, meaningful, and thought-provoking.
